Pakistani Cuisine


Learn about Pakistani Cuisine

Pakistani cuisine is an important part of Pakistani culture, which includes cuisine originating from the diverse regions of Pakistan, as well as from Pakistani people in other parts of the world. Because of the Pakistani diaspora and historical power of the country, Pakistani cuisine has influenced many other cuisines in South Asia and beyond, with modifications made to cater to local palates. Pakistani food staples such as biryani, kebabs, lentils, naan, and spices like garam masala and turmeric are now loved worldwide.
